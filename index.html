03M — The Club

A five-star club, built the way instructors wish every dive center were.

Azul runs its own training pools and a private beach entry, so lessons start in calm, controlled water before anyone touches open sea. When it's time to go further, our captains walk you straight in from the sand — no shuttling between operators, no borrowed gear, no guesswork.

It's the same principle behind the five-star rating: the pool, the shoreline, and the instruction all live under one flag, instead of scattered across separate operators.
